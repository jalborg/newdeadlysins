HOW THE QUALITIES WERE RATED

The participants were asked to choose the 7 worst and 7 best qualities from a list of 25 qualities. The rating of the qualities is based on how many people chose them.

"Insincerity" was chosen by most people, making it the worst deadly sin. Click on the words for details.
